SCHEDULING

Scheduling for both massage and sauna is done by appointment. Please call 715-778-5950 and leave a message stating your name and callback number, length of massage and/or service, and when is a good time for you (day/evening or a specific date). Because massage is typically booked out 6 weeks, we have a cancellation list that you can request to be put on as well.

Please arrive 5 minutes prior to all massage and sauna appointments so time is not taken off your session. If you are new to Breath and Bodywork, your first appointment will take a little more time. We schedule an additional 15 minutes prior in order to fill out and review paperwork, talk about massage/sauna and what you can expect during your sessions.
